About Us
What is HISC?
Hangzhou Indonesian Student Community (HISC) which is then adjusted to the name Indonesian Students' Association in The People's Republic of China Hangzhou (HISC – PPI Tiongkok Hangzhou Branch) is a student organization based in Hangzhou, Zhejiang that is officially established since 21st October 2012 in Hangzhou, China. HISC officially joined and is the branch that formed the birth of the Indonesian Students' Association in The People's Republic of China. Mandarin name for HISC - Indonesian Students' Association in The People's Republic of China, Hangzhou branch is 在华印尼学生协会杭州市.

The student organization that is based in Hangzhou, Zhejiang province, China has been officially acknowledged by the Embassy of the Republic of Indonesia in Beijing that is under the guidance of the Indonesian Students' Association in The People's Republic of China (PPI Tiongkok) named HISC-PPIT Hangzhou.
