Vision and Mission
VISION
To make Hangzhou Indonesian Community as an active organization, while developing and maintaining a harmonious connection between one member to another and also to become a forum full of aspirations for Indonesian students in Hangzhou.
MISSION
  1. Become a vessel for Indonesian students to exchange ideas , become more creative, gain more experience, and develop their potential to its fullest.
  2. Building and maintaining a good relationship with the Indonesian Student’s Association in The People’s Republic of China.
  3. Strengthening the ties among the Indonesian students community in Hangzhou.
  4. Strengthening the relationship between Hangzhou Indonesian Student Community (HISC) with alumni.
  5. Continue developing the work program of Hangzhou Indonesian Student Community (HISC) that has been carried out before.
