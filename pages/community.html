Our Community
Sanggar Wayang

Sanggar Wayang Budaya Bangsa, which is usually called Sanggar Wayang was formed in the year 2017 and is officially established in the 1st December 2018. Sanggar Wayang is a community that accommodates artistic talents for the Indonesian students in Hangzhou. Not only is there traditional dance, but also modern dance, music, orchestra, etc. Sanggar Wayang has a vision to conserve Indonesian culture until it is widely known by the people of Hangzhou, as well as the entire China.

Sports Community

Exercising is one of the most important activities that are affecting our daily life . Exercising together with your friends could also be a pleasant distraction amidst our work life. For Indonesian students out there that are studying in Hangzhou and looking for friends to exercise together, HISC has the right sports community for you. Having 6 sports communities , which includes basketball, soccer, badminton, chess, billiard, and e-Sports, we also aim to strengthen the bonds among HISC members. Come and join the fun with us!
